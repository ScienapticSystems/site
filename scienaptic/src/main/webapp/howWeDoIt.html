How It Works


Our mission is simple. We want to industrialise the use of analytics driven decision making in organizations. We have added decades of analytics experience to sophisticated technology so that the handoffs
among technology, process and humans are smoothened.

Fluid Data Management

  • Reduces friction in your data environment.
  • Enables leveraging social and other unstructured data.
  • Data access becomes simple and intuitive even for business users.

Auto Aware, Adaptive Technology

  • Our proprietary software uses machine intelligence to mimic the thought process of a smart analyst.
  • Scienaptic platform preserves the knowledge of experts and makes it available to business users and new analysts. This dramatically increases the speed and innovativeness of analysts.

Advanced Algorithm Expertise

  • Our hands-on experts help solve your most complex problems using a combination of advanced machine learning, artificial intelligence and optimization.

Visualization and Storyboarding

  • Bring alive your analyses and communicate complex concepts using the creative flow and visualization embedded in our powerful storyboarding layer.
