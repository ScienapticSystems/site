What We Do


We bring a clever combination of analytic services and technology to deliver business impact. We can do fixed scope advanced analytics projects as proof of concept. And, we can run some of your ongoing analytics processes. These persistent engagements enable us to demonstrate serious progress in your Analytics Quotient.


Our engagements have varying proportions of technology in line with your existing technology stack and needs. All of our work is oriented towards key functions in your organization.

Omni Channel CRM

Customer 360 degree view

  • Understand customer activity across channels and processes
  • Model customer behaviour in real-time to determine evolving needs across all touch points

Multi-channel customer experience

  • Enhance customer experience through social media integration and historical visit-stream patterns.
  • Synergise offers/ service actions across channels to identify optimal mapping

Marketing/ Customer service effectiveness

  • Create coherent metrics across processes to understand bottlenecks.
  • Identify reasons for process variation and solve for problems before they impact customers.

Sales force and relationship manager productivity

  • Embed intelligent analytic capability into every sales/ service interaction.
  • Build timely and effective triggers for sales force to act on opportunities / threats.

Risk Management

Risk assessment and underwriting

  • Determine exposure and quantify impact of market rate movements, credit quality changes, and operational risks.
  • Build and monitor PD, LGD and EAD scorecards.
  • Design analytically optimized underwriting strategies customer acquisition and lifecycle.
  • Create a robust test & learn environment to manage risk appetite.

Portfolio management and reporting

  • Reduce the effort required in producing established reports / dashboards.
  • Improve the existing reporting formats, augment them with drill-downs .
  • Collate cross functional internal and available external data and create additional triangulations to anticipate risks.

Regulation & compliance

  • Support model development, validation and stress testing activities to comply with CCAR / Basel provisions.
  • Demonstrate robust risk management encompassing accurate data, detailed audit trails, and metrics for effective control.
  • Create an ability to iterate quickly to respond to requests from regulators, automate to reduce effort required to do documentation.

Management of fraud/ abuse

  • Use structured/ unstructured data to anticipate vulnerabilities and detect new fraud patterns.
  • Leverage advanced machine learning and build highly targeted models to protect customers and capital.
