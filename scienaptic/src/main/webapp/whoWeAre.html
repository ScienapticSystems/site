Our Name

Scienaptic
=
Science
+
Synapse


 

Scienaptic is a neologism combining science and synapse. Science is about creating valid knowledge based on observable facts. The endeavour to help organizations make scientific decisions based on data is fundamental to our vision. Synapses enable neurons to transmit signals in the most complex computer we know, the human brain. Synapses inspire us to think of brain like lateral connections, we think those connections create step change.

In both our technology development, and the way we deliver our services, we employ the concepts of robust knowledge and multiple connections, thus bringing to life the meaning of our name to every client engagement.

Who we hire

More than anything else we are looking for high energy, passion for learning and clarity of thought. We would like you to be able to manage your ego well so that you can be effective in our non-hierarchical organization. In specific, we are looking for coders and analysts with experience between 2-15 years in the following four areas:

You would have broad and deep Java expertize either in back end algorithmic implementation or in building user experience enhancements.

You did good bit of work with relational databases but have now become a big data enthusiast. You have experimented with technologies like Hadoop / Hive, Cassandra, MongoDB or document databases.

You are technologically adept but are expert in solving business problems with data and analytics. You have applied advanced analytics to solve marketing or risk problems.

You are the top geek, you love deep maths. You have built and experimented with range of econometric, machine learning and AI algorithms.
