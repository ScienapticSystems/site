Pankaj describes himself as complex. The good news is that he thinks complexity is a way of life and managing it productively is his muse.

About 16 years ago, he was finishing up his PhD dissertation and a bit of serendipity landed him with Decision Science center of GE. Since then he has been practising and advising in the art and science of Analytics across marketing and risk functions. While his personal estimate of his hands on capability is more modest, most who have worked with him think of him as detail oriented and technically sophisticated. He does confess though that he has been a student of confluence of mind, life, science and machines for over 25 years.

Pankaj is energised by customer conversations, working with smart passionate and diverse people. After a long corporate career, these days he is totally consumed and energised about driving game changing client impact by creating a globally excellent technology and analytics company.


Muthu is a true innovator at heart and becomes restless if the same work has to be done in the same way twice.

Hailing from a business family, Muthu wanted to be a civil servant, but changed his mind when he was doing an operations research course in 1997. Fascinated with solving complex optimization problems, Muthu started out in software development for transportation before joining the GE Decision Science Centre. Since then, he has worked across multiple industries like consumer finance, commercial finance, energy, transportation and e-commerce with leading global players.

Highly process oriented, Muthu is proud of the two patents he filed for incremental sales and offer optimization in 2001, and brings the same level of creative enthusiasm to deploying a combination of analytics and technology even today.
